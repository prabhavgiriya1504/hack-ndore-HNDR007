Frequently Asked Questions

How to apply for a permit?

You can apply for permits online by visiting the 'Apply for Permits' section.

How to pay bills?

You can pay your bills online by navigating to the 'Pay Bills' section.

How to request public services?

Public service requests can be made through the 'Request Public Services' section.

How to access public records?

Public records are available in the 'Public Records' section.

How to view meeting agendas?

Meeting agendas can be found in the 'Meeting Agendas' section.

How to get government updates?

Government updates are posted in the 'Government Updates' section.

What should I do if I see a street light out?

Report street light outages in the 'Street Maintenance' section of the e-Office website or contact the municipal public works department.
